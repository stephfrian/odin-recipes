Lemonade

Lemonade's Beauty

Lemonade is so terrific because it is thirst-quenching, sweet, tart, and sour all at once. It is pretty easy to make, only requiring minimal ingredients, like lemons and sugar. Everyone needs to try lemonade at least once in their lifetime!

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups white sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 9 medium lemons, or more if necessary
  • 7 cups ice-cold water
  • ice, as needed

Steps

  1. Place sugar and 1 cup water in small saucepan. Stir the mixture until the sugar dissolves and the mix comes to a boil. Then set the mix aside.
  2. Soften lemons by rolling them. Cut each of them in half crosswise and squeeze the lemons into a liquid measuring cup. You can add pulp to your juice, but throw away any seeds. Juice the lemons until you end up with 1 1/2 cups of juice and pulp.
  3. Pour 7 cups of ice-cold water into a pitcher. Stir in the lemon juice and pulp, then finally add the simple syrup mix to taste. Add ice as needed.
  4. Enjoy!
