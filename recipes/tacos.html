Tacos

Tacos' Awesomeness

Tacos with a modern twist are great because they’re customizable and full of bold flavors. Whether it’s crispy or soft shells, seasoned ground beef, fresh lettuce, melted cheese, or a splash of salsa, each bite is a satisfying mix of textures. They’re quick to prepare, easy to enjoy, and perfect for gatherings or weeknight meals, making them a go-to comfort food for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup peanut oil
  • 12 (6-inch) yellow corn tortillas
  • Kosher
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 16 oz ground sirloin
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2/3 cup low-sodium beef broth
  • 6 oz panela cheese, crumbled
  • 12 pickled jalapeno slices
  • 1 cup shredded iceberg lettuce
  • 1 large tomato, seeded and chopped
  • 1/2 cup fresh cilantro leaves
  • 2 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tbsp ground cumin
  • 2 tsp cornstarch
  • 2 tsp kosher salt
  • 1 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 250 degrees F
  2. Heat peanut oil in a pan
  3. Fry the taco shells
  4. Cook onions until softened and lightly browned. Add meat, 1 tsp salt, and garlic. Add Taco Potion and beef broth. Bring to simmer and cook, uncovered, until sauce is slightly thickened, 2-3 minutes.
  5. Assemble each taco with meat mixture, panela, jalapeno slices, lettuce, tomatoes, and cilantro.
